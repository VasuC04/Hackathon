About Us

BizzBlock is a new gen, blockchain empowered B2C platform that provides businesses to better display their products and services to the consumers on a single platform. BizzBlock aims to host companies who provide services ranging from plumbing, AC repair to hair cut and massages at the comfort of your home. We also aim to provide small businesses a platform to run crowdfunding campaigns which will help them improve and expand their business to new horizons.
